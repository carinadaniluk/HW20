A Non-Profit Website Redesign Case Study

In this case study, I worked as a UX researcher and designer alongside 3 other UX prospective designers and researchers to redesign a desktop and mobile web website aiding individuals and families diagnosed with PKU.

User Research

Conducting research was very important to us to understand what users would be looking for when coming to a site like NSPKU.org. While our team was not able to secure an interview with someone diagnosed with PKU or their family members, we were able to secure time with two subject matter experts in the field.

To better understand the prospective users of our website, we conducted interviews with two Pharmacists that specialize in PKU to understand pain points for those impacted with PKU and hypothesize what we could do to improve the current site.
